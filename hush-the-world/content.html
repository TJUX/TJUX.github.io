Why is Hush the World white noise effective for sound anxiety relief?

Consistent volume

More info ⯈

Traditional ambient white noise often contains dips in volume or activity which can allow nuisance noises to be audible, in turn triggering your anxiety. Hush the World white noise is designed to maintain a consistent volume in order to ensure even coverage of outside noise.

Blocks out bass

More info ⯈

Most available white noise tracks cover mid to high-end sound frequencies and are ineffective at covering up deeper bass-heavy audio such as sound systems, TVs, vehicles or deep voices. Some Hush the World white noise tracks are designed to target these sounds specifically, and through the right headphones or speakers can block them out entirely.

High quality

More info ⯈

Hush the World white noise is created at the highest quality and doesn’t suffer from heavy compression such as that which occurs in YouTube videos and some streaming services. You can download lossless FLAC files which ensure the sound is at the maximum quality it was originally outputted at. This results in a much more comfortable and enjoyable white noise experience.
